Wildlife Safari Expeditions

Ethical adventures into Ethiopia’s wild heart, encounter rare endemics like the Ethiopian wolf, Gelada monkeys, and Walia ibex in dramatic landscapes.

Ethiopia’s Premier Wildlife Experiences

Discover unique highland and savanna safaris, focused on conservation, small groups, and unforgettable sightings of Africa’s rarest animals.

Simien Mountains National Park

5–8 Days

Trek the dramatic escarpments of UNESCO-listed Simien, home to endemic Gelada monkeys, Walia ibex, and the rare Ethiopian wolf.

  • UNESCO World Heritage & endemic species hotspot
  • Large troops of golden-maned Gelada baboons
  • Cliff-dwelling Walia ibex & possible Ethiopian wolf sightings
  • Camping or eco-lodges with stunning views
  • Expert local guides & ethical wildlife viewing

Bale Mountains National Park

4–7 Days

Explore the afro-alpine plateau of the Sanetti, the best place on Earth to see the critically endangered Ethiopian wolf.

  • World’s rarest canid — Ethiopian (Simien) wolf
  • Mountain nyala, giant forest hog & Bale monkey
  • High-altitude hikes & 4x4 drives on the plateau
  • Over 300 bird species including endemics
  • Conservation-focused & low-impact tourism

Awash National Park & Rift Valley

3–6 Days

Classic savanna safari near Awash River, spot oryx, greater kudu, baboons, hippos, and hundreds of bird species.

  • Big game: oryx, kudu, warthog, gazelles
  • Hippo & crocodile boat trips on lakes
  • Over 450 bird species — paradise for birders
  • Hot springs & volcanic landscapes
  • Comfortable lodges & game drives
